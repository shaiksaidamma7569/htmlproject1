OM Namo Venkatesaya 🙏 🛕

Om Namo Venkatesya is another rare film for devotees and award winning performance of Nagarjuna and terrific direction by Raghavendra

Movie Review

Om Namo Venkatesya is another rare film for devotees and award winning performance of Nagarjuna and terrific direction by Raghavendra Rao does not disappoint. Om Namo Venkatesaya (2017): India is a diverse country with number of Gods which in turn created number of religions and groups.Mythology and devotional stories of India are famous all over the world.There are very few directors who can bring those larger than life stories onto screen. K.Raghavendra rao is one of the rare ones who is expert in making devotional movies without missing any commercial elements.His Annamayya and Sri Ramadasu are considered classics and fans of both Nagarjuna and Raghavendra Rao always expect a devotional movie from them.But their last combo Shiridi Sai failed to impress critics and fans alike.Now they are back with bio-pic of Hathiram Baba,a devotee of Lord Venkateshwara, who is said to have created rules and regulations on Tirumala Hills.

Manam 👨 ‍ 👩 ‍ 👦

A Timeless Tale of Love and Rebirt

Manam is a 2014 Telugu romantic fantasy drama directed by Vikram Kumar and produced by Akkineni Nagarjuna. The film features Nageswara Rao, Nagarjuna, Naga Chaitanya, and Samantha Ruth Prabhu in pivotal roles. It is a unique film that beautifully blends romance, destiny, and reincarnation. The story revolves around a couple whose love transcends time and lifetimes. Through the concept of rebirth, the film connects three generations, showing how true love finds its way back despite obstacles. The narrative smoothly shifts between different eras, keeping the audience emotionally engaged. The performances are one of the biggest highlights of the film. The legendary Akkineni Nageswara Rao delivers a memorable performance, adding emotional depth to the story. Nagarjuna and Naga Chaitanya impress with their roles, while Samantha shines with her charm and expressive acting. The music composed by Anup Rubens enhances the film’s emotional impact. Songs like “Kanulanu Thaake” and “Piyo Piyo Re” are soulful and well-placed. The cinematography and visual effects are well-executed, giving the film a magical and elegant feel. Manam is not just a love story but a celebration of relationships, destiny, and eternal bonds. Its emotional storytelling and unique concept make it a memorable cinematic experience. Overall, Manam is a heart-touching film that proves true love is eternal and beyond time.

Dear Comerade 🤗 ✊

Learning to control his rage for their love and broader social justice.

Dear Comrade is a 2019 Telugu romantic drama directed by Bharat Kamma, starring Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna in the lead roles. The film beautifully blends intense emotions, romance, and social awareness, making it a meaningful cinematic experience. The story revolves around Bobby (Vijay Deverakonda), a hot-headed student leader who struggles to control his anger, and Lily (Rashmika Mandanna), a calm and strong woman with a painful past. Their love story is deep and emotional, but Bobby’s aggressive nature becomes a major obstacle in their relationship. The film explores themes like anger management, love, trauma, and personal growth. Vijay Deverakonda delivers a powerful and intense performance, showcasing both raw aggression and emotional vulnerability. Rashmika Mandanna gives one of her finest performances, portraying Lily with grace and strength. Their on-screen chemistry feels natural and emotionally engaging. The music composed by Justin Prabhakaran is one of the film’s biggest strengths. Songs like “O Kalala” and “Nee Satyam” enhance the emotional depth of the story. The cinematography is visually appealing, especially the Kashmir and college sequences. While the film is slow-paced in parts, its emotional honesty and strong performances make it impactful. Dear Comrade is not a typical commercial entertainer but a heartfelt story about love, healing, and self-control. Overall, Dear Comrade is a bold and emotional film that leaves a lasting impression on the audience.
